Don't believe me? Check the TLD on this website, it is a .org domain. It is a common misconception that since org is short for organization, that only verified organizations can get them. You can buy one yourself if you want, they only cost around $10.

But then how do I tell if a domain is credible?

While .org is not a restricted TLD, there are TLDs that have registration restrictions. These registration restricted domains are more credible because the entity that registered them had to verify their identity. Additionally, websites on these TLDs are significantly less likely to be scams than other domains.
